About us

Revival Bodywork guides clients to empowerment over their own health and trust in the self-healing nature of the body. Our foundation is built on anatomy, evidence based science, and the integration of the physical body with the mind and spirit.

Massage has countless therapeutic benefits and is a safe alternative or complementary option to conventional healthcare methods. Massage may help you to avoid or reduce prescription medications or invasive medical procedures. Ask your doctor if massage is a suitable addition to your wellness plan.

Wholistic approach

We offer an integrated approach to aid in the healing of the body, mind, and spirit. Classic massage strokes, deep tissue techniques, and trigger point therapy are used to manipulate the soft tissues of the body and induce deep relaxation for a complete revival

Professional service

We are Delaware Licensed Massage Therapists and insured by the American Massage Therapy Association.

DE License number: MT0004364

Personalized sessions

Every session at Revival Bodywork is unique and tailored to each individual’s needs and preferences. We do not practice with a recipe, but are able to listen to the body and adapt to according to its needs

Clean & safe

Located in a professional office building in North Wilmington, you will feel safe arriving for your first appointment and each session that follows. We surpass mandatory state hygiene procedures to ensure your safety and provide a therapeutic environment for a fully rejuvenating experience
